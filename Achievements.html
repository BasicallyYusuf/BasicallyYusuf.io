Achievements

Maseeha Sahib holds a Bachelor of Applied Sciences in Food Science and Technology, a testament to her dedication and expertise in the field. Throughout her academic journey, she acquired a comprehensive understanding of food science principles, including food chemistry, microbiology, and processing techniques. Her coursework equipped her with the knowledge and skills necessary to analyze, develop, and improve food products, ensuring their safety, quality, and nutritional value. With a solid foundation in food science and technology, Maseeha Sahib is well-equipped to tackle the challenges and complexities of the food industry. Her education not only provided her with theoretical knowledge but also practical experience through laboratory work, research projects, and industry internships. Armed with her academic qualifications, she is poised to make significant contributions to the advancement of food science and the development of innovative food products that meet the needs and preferences of consumers.
